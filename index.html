Hair Care

Healthy Hair Styles

Pulled-Out Dutch Braid

Braiding can be like marmite: you either love it or hate it. But if you’re not a fan of braids, hear us out. They are the perfect way to create a put-together style whilst protecting your hair from damage.

Half-up, half-down

Half-up, half-down is the perfect hairstyle for healthy hair. Make sure that the ponytail is placed towards the back of the head, rather than on top.

Messy High Bun

Sometimes, you need your hair completely out of your face. Whilst we know a high ponytail can cause damage, a high, loose bun is a much better option.

Low Pony Tail

This hairstyle for healthy hair doesn’t need much introduction. If you have never quickly tied your hair into a low pony, where have you been?

Skin Care

5 SKIN CARE SECRETS

Applying sunscreen to hand

Sunscreen is the closest thing we have to a fountain of youth. It really can slow down skin aging and help prevent skin cancer.

Don't smoke

Smoking speeds up how quickly your skin ages. If you smoke, your wounds will also take longer to heal. Also, research shows that smoking worsens some skin diseases.

Resist the urge to scrub your skin clean

Avoid scrubbing as it irritates your skin, which can worsen any skin condition.

Wash your face when waking and before bed

Washing when you wake up removes bacteria that settle on your face while sleeping, and before bed removes makeup and grime, which may have landed on your skin.

Gently wash your face

Wet your face it with lukewarm water. Then apply a mild cleanser in a circular motion. Finally, rinse off the cleanser and gently pat your face with a clean towel.

Mental Health Care

5 MENTAL HEALTH TIPS

1. Track gratitude and achievement with a journal:

Include 3 things you were grateful for and 3 things you were able to accomplish each day.

2. Set up a getaway:

It could be camping or an ordinary trip. The act of planning a vacation and having something to look forward to boosts your overall happiness.

3. Work your strengths:

Do something you're good at to build self-confidence, then tackle a tougher task.

4. Experiment something new:

A new recipe, write a poem, paint or try a Pinterest project; creative expression and overall well-being are linked.

5. Dance around while you do your housework:

Not only will you get chores done, but dancing reduces levels of stress hormones, and increases endorphins (the "feel-good" chemicals).

Our Message to You

The definition of beauty is varying among different people in the world. Even though almost everyone knows the term beauty, many people are struggling in defining it and persuading others to agree with their opinions. Beauty is defined by a combination of qualities existent in a person or thing that fulfills the aesthetic feels or brings about profound gratification.

Many people define beauty as a term to describe a person’s physical appearance; they often think that beauty comes from magazines, video girls, or even models. Although the term beauty can define a person’s physical appearance, true beauty lies in the way one acts and thinks rather than the way one look.
